Hello, world! Welcome to W8! Assignment

These are the requirements for the assignment:

  1. Contain at least four different .html pages, at least one of which is index.html
  2. It should be possible to get from any page on your website to any other page by following one or more hyperlinks.
  3. Use at least ten (10) distinct HTML tags besides html,head, body and title
  4. Integrate one or more features from Bootstrap into your site.
  5. Have at least one stylesheet file of your own creation, styles.css, which uses at least five (5) different CSS selectors
  6. Within each selector which you use a total of at least five (5) different CSS properties, such as font-size, or margin
  7. Integrate one or more features of JavaScript into your site to make your site more interactive.
